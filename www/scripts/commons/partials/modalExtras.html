Extras

Want to level up your Faraday usage? You can download any of the following enhancers to connect with your instance:


Faraday CLI
Link: https://github.com/infobyte/faraday-cli
Use Faraday directly from your favorite terminal. This is a common replacement for our GTK Client.

Agent Dispatcher
Link: https://github.com/infobyte/faraday_agent_dispatcher
Integrate Faraday with your scanning tools and automate all your scans. Our Dispatcher allows you to create custom integrations for your instance.

Faraday Client
DEB Link: https://storage.googleapis.com/faraday-community/faraday-client_amd64.deb
RPM Link: https://storage.googleapis.com/faraday-community/faraday-client_amd64.rpm
Run commands directly from our GTK Client and get all results imported automatically into your instance.

Methodology Templates
Link: https://github.com/infobyte/faraday_templates/tree/master/methodologies
Common security methodologies ready to be used within your Faraday projects.

Vulnerability KB Templates (CWE)
Link: https://github.com/infobyte/faraday_templates/tree/master/vulnerability_templates
Populate your Faraday KB with default CWE information.

Burp Extender
Link: https://portswigger.net/bappstore/82f3cbaea46c4f158fd85bbccc90c31c
Send findings directly from Burp into your Faraday instance. Automatically populate your project or choose exactly what you want to share.
